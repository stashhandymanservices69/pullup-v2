# 04 — Expansion Path: AU → US

Use this only after AU operations are stable.

## AU Stability Gate (before US)
- [ ] 30 days stable operations in AU
- [ ] Reconciliation mismatch rate consistently low
- [ ] Support SLA consistently met
- [ ] Refund/dispute process working without manual chaos
- [ ] Commission fraud controls active

## US Readiness Checklist
- [ ] US Terms/Privacy/Refund language prepared
- [ ] US tax configuration reviewed in accounting platform
- [ ] Stripe US payout and compliance requirements reviewed
- [ ] Regional support hours/escalation plan defined
- [ ] Fraud and chargeback thresholds tightened for expansion

## Rollout Strategy
- [ ] Start with controlled US pilot cohort
- [ ] Monitor payment success, support load, fraud indicators
- [ ] Expand only when pilot KPIs pass for 2+ weeks

## What Stripe Solves vs What You Still Own

### Stripe helps with
- [ ] Payments rails
- [ ] Merchant onboarding tooling
- [ ] Global payout infrastructure

### You still own
- [ ] Legal/policy compliance per market
- [ ] Tax configuration accuracy in your books
- [ ] Dispute handling quality and customer outcomes
- [ ] Support operations and incident response
