## Marketing Principles

### 1. Free First, Paid Later
- Exhaust free channels before spending money
- Test messaging with organic content before ads
- Pay only when you know what works

### 2. Local Over Global
- Target 30km radius (your city first)
- Word-of-mouth is powerful in cafe community
- One happy cafe refers 3 more

### 3. Show, Don't Tell
- Demo video > long explanation
- Customer testimonials > your claims
- Live orders > feature lists

### 4. Build in Public
- Share journey on LinkedIn (founders love this)
- Post metrics (transparently)
- Document challenges (authenticity builds trust)

## Crisis Marketing (If Growth Stalls)

### Scenario: Stuck at 50 Cafes for 2 Weeks

**Diagnosis:**
- Check: Are existing cafes getting orders?
- Check: Is word-of-mouth happening?
- Check: Are ads still converting?

**Possible Causes:**
1. Market saturation (all nearby cafes already signed up)
2. Poor product experience (cafes not getting orders)
3. Pricing concerns (cafes think it's too expensive)
4. Competition (someone else launched similar)

**Solutions:**
1. **Expand Radius:** Target next city (30-50km away)
2. **Improve Activation:** Call each inactive cafe, personally help them
3. **Price Promotion:** "Next 50 cafes get 60 days free SMS"
4. **Feature Launch:** Add highly requested feature, promote heavily
