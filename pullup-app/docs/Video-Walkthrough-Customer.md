# Pull Up Coffee — Customer Video Walkthrough Script

> **Duration:** ~3 minutes  
> **Tone:** Warm, casual, modern — like a friend showing you a new app  
> **Music:** Lo-fi / acoustic coffee-shop vibe (royalty-free)  
> **Format:** Screen recording on iPhone + voiceover  

---

## SCENE 1 — The Hook (0:00 – 0:12)

**[VISUAL]** Aerial/POV shot of someone pulling into a street parking spot near a coffee shop. Engine off. Phone comes out.

**[VOICEOVER]**  
> "What if your street parking spot… was a drive-thru?"  

**[VISUAL]** Phone screen shows pullupcoffee.com loading — the Pull Up Coffee landing page with the dark hero image, logo, and "Order Now" button.

**[VOICEOVER]**  
> "No app download. No queue. Just Pull Up."

---

## SCENE 2 — Landing Page (0:12 – 0:28)

**[VISUAL]** Finger taps around the landing page. Show the footer links: Order Coffee, About Vision, FAQ, Support the Founder, Affiliate.

**[VOICEOVER]**  
> "Pull Up Coffee is a web platform — works on any phone, any browser. No sign-up needed to order."

**[VISUAL]** Tap "Order Now" — transitions to the Discovery screen.

**[VOICEOVER]**  
> "Tap 'Order Now' and we'll find cafes near you."

## SCENE 3 — Discovery / Find a Cafe (0:28 – 0:55)

**[VISUAL]** Discovery screen loads. Location permission popup appears → tap "Allow". GPS locates user. Cafe cards appear with distance, name, and status badges (Open / Closed).

**[VOICEOVER]**  
> "We use your GPS to find Pull Up partner cafes nearby. You'll see their distance, whether they're open, and their logo."

**[VISUAL]** Show the search bar filtering by suburb or postcode. Adjust the radius slider.

**[VOICEOVER]**  
> "You can search by suburb or postcode, and adjust the radius if you want to see more options."

**[VISUAL]** Tap on an open cafe card.

**[VOICEOVER]**  
> "Tap a cafe to see their menu."

---

## SCENE 4 — Cafe Menu (0:55 – 1:25)

**[VISUAL]** Cafe Menu screen: cafe logo, name, "Save Favourite" button. Menu items listed — Flat White $4.50, Espresso $3.80, Cappuccino $4.80, etc.

**[VOICEOVER]**  
> "Each cafe sets their own menu and prices. Tap any item to customize it."

**[VISUAL]** Tap "Flat White" — Product Modal opens. Show size selector (Regular / Medium +$0.50 / Large +$1.00), milk options (Full Cream, Oat +$0.50, Almond, Soy), extra shot toggle (+$0.50), syrup, notes field. Tap "Add to Order".

**[VOICEOVER]**  
> "Choose your size, milk, extra shots, syrups — all priced transparently. Add notes like 'extra hot' if you want."

**[VISUAL]** Cart counter appears at bottom — shows "1" item. Add another item (Iced Latte). Cart shows "2" with running total. Tap "Review & Pay".

**[VOICEOVER]**  
> "Add as many items as you like. When you're ready, tap 'Review & Pay'."

---

## SCENE 5 — Checkout / Vehicle Specs (1:25 – 2:05)

**[VISUAL]** Checkout screen titled "Vehicle Specs." Fields shown: Your Name, Mobile, Car Make, Color, License Plate.

**[VOICEOVER]**  
> "This is how the barista finds you. Enter your name, car details, and licence plate."

**[VISUAL]** Show the "Share live GPS" toggle. Show the "Save details for next time" checkbox. Show optional parking spot info textarea and camera button.

**[VOICEOVER]**  
> "You can share your live GPS so the barista knows exactly when you're arriving. Save your details so next time it's one-tap. You can even snap a photo of your parking spot."

**[VISUAL]** Scroll down to Order Summary — itemized list, subtotal, curbside fee ($2.00), payment processing, total. Terms checkbox.

**[VOICEOVER]**  
> "Review your order. There's a small curbside delivery fee and standard payment processing — all shown upfront. No hidden costs."

**[VISUAL]** Tick the terms checkbox. "PAY & PULL UP" button activates. Tap it.

**[VOICEOVER]**  
> "Accept the terms, and tap 'Pay & Pull Up'. You'll be taken to Stripe's secure checkout."

**[VISUAL]** Stripe Checkout page opens (card entry). Complete payment.

## Production Notes

- **Screen Recording:** Use iPhone screen recorder at 60fps, portrait mode
- **Transitions:** Smooth cross-fades between screens (0.3s)
- **Annotations:** Subtle orange highlight circles / arrows on key UI elements
- **Call-to-Action:** End card with QR code linking to pullupcoffee.com
- **Captions:** Add subtitles for accessibility (white text, dark background strip)
- **Length Control:** Keep each scene tight — if over 3 min, trim the Discovery and Checkout scenes
