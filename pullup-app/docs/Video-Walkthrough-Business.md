# Pull Up Coffee — Business Owner Video Walkthrough Script

> **Duration:** ~5 minutes  
> **Tone:** Professional but approachable — "let me show you how easy this is"  
> **Music:** Upbeat lo-fi / modern acoustic (royalty-free)  
> **Format:** Screen recording on laptop + phone side-by-side where needed

## APPENDIX: Dashboard Tab Summary

| Tab | Purpose | Key Features |
|-----|---------|-------------|
| **Orders** | Live order management | Accept/Reject, status progression, live GPS distance, customer car details |
| **History** | Past order analytics | Daily/Weekly/Monthly/Archive filters, per-order revenue breakdown |
| **Menu** | Menu management | Add/edit/delete items, photo upload, toggle active/inactive, global add-on pricing |
| **Operations** | Business controls | Online/Offline toggle, operating hours, curbside fee, notification sounds, favourite SMS alerts |
| **Payments** | Stripe & payouts | Connect bank, payout frequency, fee breakdown, early adopter status |
| **Account** | Profile settings | Business name, logo, billing email, sign out |
| **Support** | Help & AI assistant | AI chatbot, quick prompts, FAQ links |
| **Platform** | Admin-only analytics | Total cafes, orders today, gross revenue, platform-wide stats (visible to platform admins only) |

## Production Notes

- **Screen Recording:** Desktop (Chrome, 1920x1080) for dashboard scenes; iPhone (portrait) for signup/login
- **Split-Screen Technique:** For Scene 6, show laptop dashboard on left and customer's phone on right simultaneously
- **Transitions:** Clean wipe transitions between tabs; cross-fade between scenes
- **Annotations:** Orange highlight circles on buttons being tapped; brief text callouts for key features
- **B-Roll Ideas:** Barista making coffee, car pulling up to curb, phone notification close-up
- **Call-to-Action:** End card with QR code → pullupcoffee.com signup page
- **Captions:** Required — white text on semi-transparent dark bar at bottom
- **Length Control:** If over 5 min, consolidate Scenes 7+8 into one scene, trim the signup form walkthrough
