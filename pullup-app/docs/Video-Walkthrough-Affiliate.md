# Pull Up Coffee — Affiliate Video Walkthrough Script

> **Duration:** ~1.5 minutes  
> **Tone:** Quick, exciting, "easy money" energy  
> **Format:** Screen recording on phone (portrait mode)  

---

## SCENE 1 — The Hook (0:00 – 0:10)

**[VISUAL]** Text on screen over coffee-shop b-roll: "Earn 25% commission. No selling. No inventory."

**[VOICEOVER]**  
> "Know a cafe owner? Earn 25% of every curbside fee for their first 30 days — just by sharing a link."

---

## SCENE 2 — Sign Up (0:10 – 0:35)

**[VISUAL]** Open pullupcoffee.com → scroll to footer → tap "Affiliate (25% first month)" under "Earn" section.

**[VOICEOVER]**  
> "Go to pullupcoffee.com. Scroll down and tap 'Affiliate' in the footer."

**[VISUAL]** Affiliate signup form appears inside the modal. Fill in: name, email, phone, country, channels (social media, in-person, etc.), preferred referral code.

**[VOICEOVER]**  
> "Fill in your details. Pick your preferred referral code — something memorable like PULLUP-YOURNAME."

**[VISUAL]** Tap "Apply as Affiliate". Success message shows the generated code: PULLUP-ASHLEY-1234.

**[VOICEOVER]**  
> "Done. You get your unique referral code instantly. No approval wait."

---

## SCENE 3 — Share & Earn (0:35 – 0:55)

**[VISUAL]** Show texting a cafe owner: "Hey! You should check out Pull Up Coffee — curbside orders for your cafe. Use my code PULLUP-ASHLEY-1234 when you sign up. pullupcoffee.com"

**[VOICEOVER]**  
> "Share your code with cafe owners. When they sign up and enter your code, you're automatically linked. For every curbside order their customers place in the first 30 days, you earn 25% of the platform fee."

**[VISUAL]** Quick math overlay: "5 orders/day × $0.10 commission × 30 days = $15/cafe. Refer 10 cafes = $150/month."

---

## SCENE 4 — Dashboard (0:55 – 1:20)

**[VISUAL]** Back on pullupcoffee.com → footer → Affiliate tab → scroll down to dashboard login. Enter email + referral code. Dashboard loads.

**[VOICEOVER]**  
> "Track your earnings anytime. Enter your email and code to see your dashboard."

**[VISUAL]** Dashboard shows: Total Earned, Pending, Paid Out, Active Cafes. List of referred cafes. Recent commissions.

**[VOICEOVER]**  
> "See every cafe you've referred, every commission earned, and when you'll be paid."

---

## SCENE 5 — Closing (1:20 – 1:30)

**[VISUAL]** Back to landing page. Tagline: "Refer cafes. Earn cash. Help local business."

**[VOICEOVER]**  
> "No selling. No inventory. Just share a code and earn. Sign up at pullupcoffee.com."

**[TEXT ON SCREEN]**  
```
pullupcoffee.com
Scroll to footer → "Affiliate"
```

---

## Production Notes
- Keep it under 90 seconds — attention span for "how to earn" content is short
- Use upbeat music (lo-fi or acoustic)
- Record on phone in portrait (most people will watch on Instagram/TikTok)
- Add captions — most social video is watched muted
