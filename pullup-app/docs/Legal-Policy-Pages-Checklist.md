# Legal Policy Pages Checklist (Production)

Use this when publishing legal pages to production.

## Required Pages

- Terms of Use
- Privacy Policy
- Cookie Notice
- Refund and Dispute Policy
- Cafe Partner Terms
- Intellectual Property and Brand Use Policy
- Contact and Legal Notice

## Link Placement

- Footer links visible on all public pages
- Signup flow links before account creation
- Checkout flow links before payment confirmation
- Cafe onboarding links before acceptance

## Records and Evidence

- Save the exact version text and publication date
- Save screenshot/PDF of each published page
- Keep version history in repository and counsel folder

## Change Management

- Define effective date on each page
- Maintain changelog section for policy updates
- Trigger re-acceptance for material Terms changes

## Minimum Enforcement Wording to Include

- ownership of platform IP and brand assets
- no scraping, no reverse engineering, no unauthorized automation
- no re-hosting, copying, or derivative commercial use without consent
- right to suspend/terminate for misuse
- governing law and jurisdiction clause
